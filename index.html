About HITEC University

HITEC (Heavy Industries Taxila Education City) University is a leading educational institution in Pakistan, committed to excellence in teaching, learning, and research.

Established in 2007 as a project of Heavy Industries Taxila (HIT), HITEC University has evolved into a center of academic excellence with a strong focus on engineering, computer science, and management sciences.

Located in Taxila, a city with rich historical and cultural heritage, the university provides a conducive environment for intellectual growth and holistic development of students.
